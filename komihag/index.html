Komihåg

Komihåg helps you learn and remember things you read.

Start by selecting any text that you find interesting and want to remember, share the text with the Komihåg app and the app will use AI to transform the shared text into a flashcard.

Launch the Komihåg app to scroll through your decks or create a widget that displays your cards continuously, so that you are exposed to the things you want to learn with minimal effort from you.

Komihåg is an app developed and maintained by Last Epoch AB
